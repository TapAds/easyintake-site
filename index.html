AI Intake Use Cases

Easy Intake transforms unstructured Spanish (or any language) input into structured English output for legal, medical, financial, and insurance systems. Qualification, data extraction, compliance structuring — not just translation.

Mass Tort & Class Action Intake

High-volume intake for silicosis, asbestos, defective devices, pesticides. AI captures exposure history, employer details, symptoms timeline — often messy in Spanish — and normalizes into claim-ready format.

Immigration Legal Screening

Spanish intake into English forms (I-485, I-589). Eligibility screening for asylum, U visa, T visa. AI routes to DIY or attorney based on complexity.

Personal Injury (Auto & Workplace)

Spanish-speaking accident victims often avoid lawyers due to language barriers. AI captures accident details, injuries, insurance info and auto-generates case summaries.

Life & Health Insurance Intake

For undocumented or mixed-status families. AI collects health info, beneficiaries, income in Spanish and normalizes for carrier underwriting.

Auto & Property Insurance Claims

Voice → structured claim data. Damage intake, loss inventory. Critical for Spanish-speaking drivers and property owners in high-risk regions.

Tenant Maintenance & Housing Issues

Tenants report via voice or SMS ("no hay agua caliente"). AI converts to structured maintenance tickets in English. Habitability claims: mold, unsafe housing.

Healthcare & Medicaid / ACA Enrollment

Pre-visit intake for clinics and FQHCs. Medicaid/ACA: household info, income, eligibility screening in Spanish. TurboTax for healthcare enrollment.

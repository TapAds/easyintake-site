EasyIntake App

Last Updated: March 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Easy Intake App ("EasyIntake", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you access or use the EasyIntake App website, software platform, APIs, and related services (collectively, the "Service").

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Overview

EasyIntake App is a workflow automation and data intake platform used by businesses, professionals, and organizations to collect and manage information from their customers, clients, or applicants.

Because the platform operates in a B2B2C model, there are two types of users:

Business Users

Organizations or professionals who use EasyIntake to collect information.

End Users

Individuals who provide information through forms or workflows created by Business Users.

Business Users control how they collect and use End User data. EasyIntake processes that data on behalf of Business Users.
